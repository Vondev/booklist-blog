Our Solution : Booklist

An in-depth analysis of this opportunity.

Booklist

Discussing the effectual cycle is important to expand on the group synergy and understand why the group was efficient and ideal for this specific project. It all begins with the individuals as themselves and what personal resources they have. These resources are divided into three different categories: who the person is, what knowledge the person has and what individuals does the person know. With the individual resources, each one of the members of the team can achieve different things depending on the first box. Each one should have a goal to go after.

In our case, the “interactive with people we know” happened repeatedly both when we first interacted with each as a group to generate ideas, and when we interview people during market research. Then we pooled our resources together and now we actually started working towards a goal as a team as opposed to individuals. The pooling of resources, especially knowledge and who we are was very useful to find our goal (in our case a topic to explore by understanding topics we all are interested in).
